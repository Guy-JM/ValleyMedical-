WEIGHT LOSS PROGRAM

Our 12-Week Medical Weight Loss Program is designed to help you achieve lasting results through a comprehensive and personalized approach. The program begins with a one-on-one consultation and a detailed body composition analysis to assess your unique needs. Weekly check-ins ensure progress tracking and support, complemented by vitamin B12 injections to boost metabolism and energy levels. We incorporate FDA-approved treatments such as Semaglutide, Tirzepatide, and HCG—proven to aid in appetite control, fat loss, and improved insulin sensitivity. With expert guidance and medical supervision, we provide the tools to help you achieve sustainable weight loss and better overall health.

SLEEP STUDIES

We offer in-home sleep studies using advanced ResMed technology to evaluate Obstructive Sleep Apnea (OSA) and determine the need for CPAP therapy. This convenient, at-home test monitors breathing patterns, oxygen levels, and sleep quality, providing a comprehensive analysis without the need for an overnight stay at a clinic. Our specialists review your results and, if necessary, guide you through the process of starting CPAP treatment to improve sleep quality and overall health.
